# Plans & Pricing

InfraMap offers flexible pricing plans to accommodate teams of all sizes, from individual developers to large enterprises. All plans include secure, read-only access to your infrastructure with no credential storage required.

## Free Forever

Perfect for side projects and small AWS accounts. Get started with InfraMap at no cost.

**Pricing:** Free

**Includes:**
- 1 seat
- 1 AWS account
- 1 AWS region
- Unlimited resources
- Daily scans
- Core infrastructure map
- Basic cost view
- Basic drift detection

## Pro

Designed for teams seeking full visibility into cost and risk across multiple accounts and regions.

**Pricing:** $49/month (1 seat included, additional seats at $10/month each)

**Includes everything in Free, plus:**
- Up to 3 AWS accounts
- Up to 5 AWS regions
- Up to 5 team members
- Hourly scans (faster updates)
- Full cost analysis and findings
- Email alerts
- Complete drift detection
- Advanced cost insights

<Info>
Additional team members can be added at $10 per seat per month. Perfect for small to medium teams managing multiple AWS accounts.
</Info>

## Enterprise

**Pricing:** Custom pricing (contact us)

## Choosing the Right Plan

**Start with Free Forever if:**
- You're working on a side project or personal AWS account
- You only need to monitor a single AWS account and region
- You want to explore InfraMap's core features

**Upgrade to Pro if:**
- You're managing multiple AWS accounts or regions
- You need faster scan updates (hourly vs daily)
- You want full cost analysis and email alerts
- You're working with a small team (up to 5 members)

**Choose Enterprise if:**
- You're managing an AWS Organization with many accounts
- You need SSO/SAML for security compliance
- You require audit logs and advanced reporting
- You need priority support and custom integrations
- You want to ingest Cost and Usage Reports (CUR) for advanced cost analytics

## Upgrade or Downgrade Anytime

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated accordingly.

<Warning>
Downgrading from Pro or Enterprise to Free Forever may result in loss of access to some features or data. Make sure to export any important data before downgrading.
</Warning>
